Album Discography
"I Brought You My Bullets", 2002

Collection of post-hardcore, basement show-esque songs. Raw, rough, and criminally underrated.

"Three Cheers for Sweet Revenge", 2004

Angsty concept album about a man, a woman, and a thousand evil men. An emo spin on the American gothic genre.

"The Black Parade", 2006

Grandiose rock opera about the life of a dying cancer patient. Their magnum opus and one of the greatest albums of all time.

"Danger Days", 2010

Adrenaline-pumped story (with a spin-off comic series) about outlaws in an apocalyptic future. A huge, fun change in sound.
